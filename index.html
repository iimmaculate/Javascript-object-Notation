INSIGHTS DERIVED FROM MY VISUALIZATIONS

  • Line charts and bar charts can reveal upward, downward trends in data
  • Charts like pie charts show how data is distributed across categories.
  • Compare datasets to identify differences and similarities

CHALLENGES FOUND

  • Charts might not look the same on all browsers.
  • Big datasets and complex charts can make the page slow.
  • Charts may not fit well on small screens.

THEIR SOLUTIONS

  • Use chart tools like Chart.js that work on most browsers and test your charts on different browsers.
  • Use smaller datasets and load data in parts if it's too big.
  • Use chart tools that adjust automatically
